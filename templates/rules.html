Rules and Confirmation

Each Week:
  • Pick one team to win. Ties are losses.
  • Submit your pick by the deadline shown.
  • Picks will lock at the deadline shown.
  • Each team can only be used once.

If technical issues, email your pick to picks@fbsurvivor.com.
Payouts:
  • Payouts are dependent on participation!
  • Survivor: at least 7x the buy in.
  • Best Record: 2-4x the buy in.
  • 2nd Best Record: 1-2x the buy in.
  • If there are ties, players will split the corresponding payouts.
