Rules

Each Week:
Pick one team to win. Ties are losses.
Submit your pick by the deadlines shown.
Picks will lock at the deadlines shown.

Payouts:
Payouts are dependent on participation!
Survivor: at least 7x the buy in.
Best Record: 2-4x the buy in.
2nd Best Record: 1-2x the buy in.
Players will split the corresponding payouts for ties.

Having issues? Email your pick to picks@fbsurvivor.com.
